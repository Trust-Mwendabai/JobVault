About JobVault

Built in Zambia, for Zambia — connecting professionals with opportunities that shape careers.

Zambia's Most Trusted Job Platform

JobVault was founded in Lusaka with a simple mission: make it easier for talented Zambians to find meaningful work, and for great companies to find the people they need to grow.

We centralise job listings from across every industry, verify employer profiles, and surface the most relevant opportunities — so job seekers spend less time searching and more time building their careers.

From fresh graduates in Ndola to senior executives in Lusaka, JobVault serves professionals at every stage of their journey.
